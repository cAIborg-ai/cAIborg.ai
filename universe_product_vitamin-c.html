Vitamin C That Actually Reaches Your Cells

Most vitamin C is only partially absorbed. The rest passes through unused, providing no benefit. When it comes to immunity, energy, and cellular protection, what matters isn't just dosage — it's the form and how it's delivered to the cell.

Universe Vitamins™ Liposomal Vitamin C is a high-efficacy formula with enhanced bioavailability via phospholipid delivery system. Each shot contains 900mg vitamin C as sodium ascorbate (gentle bioavailable form) plus citrus bioflavonoids, Quercefit® quercetin from Indena (Italy), and rosehip extract.

Liposomal technology provides more efficient cellular penetration of vitamin C, amplifying its action. Vitamin C is one of the body's key antioxidants, but its effectiveness depends entirely on form and absorption.
