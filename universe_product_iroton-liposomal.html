About This Formula

This isn't just iron — it's a thoughtful absorption system that actually works. When it comes to restoring iron levels, energy, and oxygen exchange, what matters isn't just dosage — it's the form of iron, its transport in the body, and combination with cofactors.

Iron is a key element for hemoglobin synthesis, oxygen transport, brain function, and energy production. Iron deficiency can lead to chronic fatigue, reduced concentration, and weakened immunity.

Universe Vitamins™ Liposomal Iroton is a synergistic formula based on highly bioavailable iron bisglycinate, which:

  • • Gentle on the stomach
  • • High absorption rate
  • • No pronounced side effects typical of other iron forms

The formula is enhanced with key components for maximum absorption and efficacy:

  • • Vitamin C (sodium ascorbate) — increases iron absorption
  • • L-methylfolate (Quatrefolic®) — active B9 for hematopoiesis support
  • • Vitamin B6 (P-5-P) and B2 (R-5-P) — active forms for metabolism and energy
  • • L-lysine — amino acid that improves iron transport and absorption
  • • Liposomal-active phospholipid matrix — supports active substance delivery and cell membrane protection
