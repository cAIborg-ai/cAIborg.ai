Iron That Actually Works

This isn't just iron — it's a thoughtful absorption system that actually works. When it comes to restoring iron levels, energy, and oxygen exchange, what matters isn't just dosage — it's the form of iron, its transport in the body, and combination with cofactors.

Iron is a key element for hemoglobin synthesis, oxygen transport, brain function, and energy production. Iron deficiency leads to chronic fatigue, reduced concentration, and weakened immunity.

Universe Vitamins™ Liposomal Iroton uses iron bisglycinate — a form that's gentle on the stomach, has high absorption rate, and doesn't cause the side effects typical of cheaper iron forms. Enhanced with vitamin C, Quatrefolic® methylfolate, active B6 and B2, and L-lysine.
