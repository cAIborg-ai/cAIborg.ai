About This Formula

This isn't just magnesium — it's a thoughtful formula where what matters isn't just dosage, but form, composition purity, and physiological absorption.

When it comes to the nervous system, sleep, heart health, energy, and stress resilience, what matters is not just the amount of magnesium — but its form, and the absence of unnecessary tablet components that can reduce bioavailability and burden the body.

Universe Vitamins™ Magnesium Pro combines three bioavailable forms of magnesium:

  • • Magnesium citrate — quickly absorbed, supports overall magnesium levels
  • • Magnesium bisglycinate — gentle form with high bioavailability, promotes nervous system relaxation
  • • Magnesium taurate — supports cardiovascular and nervous system function

Special attention is given to tablet composition: no titanium dioxide (TiO₂), no polyvinylpyrrolidone (PVP), no polyethylene glycol (PEG) — only the necessary components, no excessive additives.
