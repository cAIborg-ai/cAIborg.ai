About This Formula

An innovative formula for methylation support, brain function, and energy metabolism — every component clinically validated.

When it comes to concentration, energy, stress resilience, nervous system and heart health — what matters isn't just vitamins, but their active forms and synergy with amino acids and minerals.

Methylation is one of the body's key biochemical processes, affecting:

  • • Brain function and cognitive performance
  • • Neurotransmitter synthesis
  • • Energy levels
  • • Detoxification and cellular protection

Methylation plays a critical role in controlling homocysteine levels — an amino acid associated with increased cardiovascular risk, cognitive impairment, and accelerated aging when elevated.
