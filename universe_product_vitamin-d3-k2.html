The D3+K2 Synergy

Universe Vitamins™ Liposomal Vitamin D3+K2 is a premium formula for immune, bone, and cardiovascular support — with maximum bioavailability.

Vitamin D3 (Quali-D®) promotes calcium absorption from the gut and supports immunity. Vitamin K2 (K2VITAL® MK-7) directs that calcium specifically to bones — and prevents arterial deposition.

Together they work significantly more effectively than separately. Calcium without K2 risks cardiovascular calcification — that's why this synergy matters.
