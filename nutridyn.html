About NutriDyn

NutriDyn was founded on our family’s passion for nutraceuticals—a passion that runs three generations deep. After seeing firsthand the remarkable results of nutrition‑based interventions and other non‑pharmaceutical methods, our founders David and Greg Peterson created NutriDyn to bring these innovative formulas exclusively to you—our practitioners—and your patients around the globe. When you partner with NutriDyn, you’re part of the family.

Our Mission

In a market where healthcare practitioners are often overlooked, we strive to be a trusted, transparent source of supplements and support for you and your practice, giving both you and your patients an improved quality of life.

На рынке, где практикующих врачей часто упускают из виду, мы стремимся быть надежным и прозрачным источником пищевых добавок и поддержки для вас и вашей практики, повышая качество жизни как для вас, так и для ваших пациентов.

Quality You Can Trust

From initial research to production and delivery, our products are tested every step of the way to ensure they live up to the impeccable standards you set for your practice. As your partner in health, we put the same level of care into ensuring quality as you do in treating each of your patients.
