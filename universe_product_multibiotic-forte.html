About This Formula

This isn't just a probiotic — it's a comprehensive microbiome support system. What matters isn't just the strains, but their count, stability, protection, and synergy with additional components.

When it comes to gut health, immunity, overall well-being, and longevity — what matters isn't merely the presence of probiotics, but their dosage, strain diversity, and the ability to reach the gut in active form.

The microbiome is a key body system influencing digestion, immunity, metabolism, and even cognitive function. Microflora imbalance can lead to weakened immunity, GI problems, and chronic inflammation.
